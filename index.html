TETRIS

Instructions


1. Make rows by changing the orientations of tetrominoes that are descending from the top or the container. You can change them by pressing the up button.

2. Said rows should be solid, no gaps.

3. Clearing a row would give you 10 points. Obtaining 100 points will result in a win.

4. Don't let the shapes reach the top of the container. Otherwise, you will lose and the game will be over.
